## 🚀 About Me

I'm a **Senior DevOps & Cloud Automation Engineer** with **8+ years** of experience designing, automating, and securing cloud infrastructure at scale. I live in the intersection of reliability, security, and speed — turning complex infrastructure challenges into elegant, automated solutions.

- 🏗️ **Infrastructure Whisperer** — I speak fluent Terraform, Ansible, and YAML (sometimes in my sleep)
- ☁️ **Multi-Cloud Native** — AWS is home, but Azure & GCP are vacation spots I know well
- 🔐 **Security Obsessed** — CyberArk, HashiVault, OPA & Checkov are my daily companions
- 🤖 **Automation Addict** — If I have to do it twice, I automate it. Period.
- 🐳 **Container Craftsman** — Kubernetes clusters don't scare me; they excite me
